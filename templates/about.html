Who We Are

We're are a small team of run of the mill guys that love to eat healthy, train well and inspire each other. We are not a massive organization, we came up with the idea to appeal to people just like you to take the hassle out of meal times with quick and easy recipes to follow. It can be overwhelming counting macros, wondering if you need a massive bank balance to eat well (or investing in Tupperware shares!) so, we have developed this app to allow you to create your own profile, upload and share your recipes on here for others to follow and hopefully do the same. We are also looking in to adding more features for you in the near future but for the mean time we've included what you need to maintain your physical and mental health through connection to other like minded guys on the same wavelength.
